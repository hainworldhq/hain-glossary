# HAINIZE

## Definition

HAINIZE is the toolkit and developer-oriented direction within the HAIN framework.

It represents the practical layer through which reusable assets, templates, and implementation-oriented materials may emerge.

## Role in the framework

HAINIZE connects the HAIN concept to practical use.

It is not yet positioned as a full product platform. Instead, it serves as the naming layer for starter assets and future tooling direction.

## Relationship to other layers

If HAIN World introduces and HAINOA explains, HAINIZE points toward practical entry.

## In one line

**HAINIZE is the toolkit and developer-oriented entry within the HAIN framework.**
