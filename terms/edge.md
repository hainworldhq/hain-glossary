# Edge

## Definition

Within the HAIN framework, edge refers to local or near-local execution environments where intelligent functions operate closer to the point of use.

## Role in the framework

Edge matters because latency, locality, privacy, resilience, and deployment realism increasingly shape how AI systems are designed.

In HAIN, edge is treated as a core structural environment rather than as a secondary optimization layer.

## In one line

**Edge is the locality and execution-near-use layer within the HAIN system landscape.**
