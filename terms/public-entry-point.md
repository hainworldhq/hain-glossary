# Public Entry Point

## Definition

A public entry point is the primary surface through which a framework, concept, or system is introduced to external audiences.

It is where first contact happens.

## Role in HAIN

Within the HAIN system, HAIN World functions as the public entry point.

Its job is to make the broader framework understandable and navigable without requiring immediate access to deeper architecture or implementation layers.

## Why it matters

Without a clear public entry point, a framework can feel fragmented or inaccessible.

A public entry point provides a stable place for first explanation, initial trust, and structured discovery.

## In one line

**A public entry point is the main external surface through which a framework is first introduced and understood.**
