# Umbrella Identity

## Definition

An umbrella identity is the top-level public identity that holds together multiple layers, assets, or subcomponents within a broader framework or system.

## Role in HAIN

Within the HAIN structure, HAIN World functions as the umbrella identity.

It connects the main site, the architecture layer, the toolkit direction, and related public-facing assets without forcing them into one undifferentiated label.

## Why it matters

As a framework expands, it needs a stable surface through which different layers can still be presented as part of one coherent system.

An umbrella identity makes that possible by providing breadth without eliminating internal structure.

## In one line

**An umbrella identity is the top-level public layer that holds together multiple connected parts of a broader system.**
