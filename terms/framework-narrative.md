# Framework Narrative

## Definition

A framework narrative is the structured story through which a framework explains what it is, why it exists, how it is organized, and where it may lead.

It is the public logic of the system expressed as narrative.

## Role in HAIN

In HAIN, the framework narrative connects definition, structure, terminology, websites, and public-facing materials into one coherent explanation.

It allows the system to be presented as more than isolated labels or technical fragments.

## Why it matters

A framework may have strong architecture and good terminology, but still remain difficult to communicate if it lacks narrative coherence.

Framework narrative helps turn structure into something legible, transferable, and publicly meaningful.

## In one line

**A framework narrative is the structured story that explains what a framework is, why it exists, and how its parts relate.**
