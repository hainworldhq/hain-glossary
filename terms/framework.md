# Framework

## Definition

Within the HAIN system, a framework is a structured conceptual layer used to organize language, architecture logic, and public-facing interpretation around a coherent subject.

It is more than a theme and less than a fixed product system.

## Role in HAIN

HAIN is positioned as a framework rather than as a single product, platform, or standard.

This matters because the purpose of HAIN is not only to deliver one implementation. Its purpose is to create a clearer structure for understanding and explaining AI-native systems across cloud, edge, and agentic contexts.

## Why it matters

A framework provides:

- conceptual order
- clearer boundaries
- reusable language
- room for future extension

Without a framework, related ideas may exist, but they often remain fragmented.

## In one line

**A framework is a structured conceptual system that organizes how a subject is explained, interpreted, and extended.**
