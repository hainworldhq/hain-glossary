# HAIN

## Definition

HAIN stands for **Hybrid AI Native**.

It is the core framework concept that describes AI systems across cloud, edge, and agentic environments through clearer public framing, cleaner architectural language, and structured narrative assets.

## Role in the framework

HAIN is the top-level conceptual layer.

It is not a standalone product name. It is the underlying framework identity from which HAIN World, HAINOA, and HAINIZE are organized.

## In one line

**HAIN is the core framework concept behind the Hybrid AI Native system.**
