# Principles

## Definition

Principles are the foundational statements that guide how a framework is understood, interpreted, and applied.

They are not implementation details. They express the deeper logic or orientation of a system.

## Role in HAIN

Within the HAIN framework, principles help explain why the structure exists and how its layers should be understood.

They provide a stable interpretive base beneath public identity, architecture logic, and toolkit direction.

## Why it matters

A framework without principles often becomes reactive, inconsistent, or overly dependent on surface language.

Principles help preserve coherence across documentation, websites, architecture notes, and future extensions.

## In one line

**Principles are the foundational statements that guide how a framework is understood and applied.**
