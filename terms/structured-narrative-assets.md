# Structured Narrative Assets

## Definition

Structured narrative assets are organized public-facing materials that help explain a framework consistently across websites, repositories, documents, and external channels.

## Role in the framework

In HAIN, structured narrative assets include overview pages, whitepaper documents, glossary entries, public descriptions, and other materials that shape how the framework is understood.

They are not only marketing content. They are part of the framework’s explanatory infrastructure.

## Why it matters

A framework becomes harder to maintain when each surface explains it differently.

Structured narrative assets create consistency, reduce drift, and make the system easier to present, extend, and reference.

## In one line

**Structured narrative assets are the organized explanation materials that keep a framework legible across public surfaces.**
