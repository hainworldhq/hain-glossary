# HAIN World

## Definition

HAIN World is the public-facing identity of the HAIN framework.

It serves as the umbrella layer through which the framework is introduced, explained, and made publicly accessible.

## Role in the framework

HAIN World is the outward-facing layer.

Its role is to connect websites, narrative materials, repositories, and public entry points into a coherent visible surface.

## Relationship to other layers

- HAIN World introduces
- HAINOA explains
- HAINIZE enables

## In one line

**HAIN World is the public entry point and umbrella identity of the HAIN framework.**
