# Conceptual Structure

## Definition

A conceptual structure is the organized relationship between the main ideas, layers, and roles within a framework.

It defines how the system is mentally arranged before it becomes a technical implementation or product form.

## Role in HAIN

In HAIN, conceptual structure is what allows HAIN World, HAINOA, and HAINIZE to function as distinct but connected layers.

It provides the internal order that prevents the framework from collapsing into a single label or a loose collection of ideas.

## Why it matters

A framework may have strong ideas but still remain unclear if their relationships are not structured.

Conceptual structure makes a framework easier to explain, easier to extend, and easier to keep coherent over time.

## In one line

**A conceptual structure is the organized relationship between the core ideas and roles within a framework.**
