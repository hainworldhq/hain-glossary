# HAINOA

## Definition

HAINOA stands for **Hybrid AI Native Open Architecture**.

It is the open architecture layer within the HAIN framework.

## Role in the framework

HAINOA provides the structural core of the framework.

It is where principles, architectural logic, conceptual hierarchy, and system interpretation become more explicit.

## Relationship to other layers

HAINOA sits beneath HAIN World and alongside HAINIZE.

Its function is not public branding or tooling delivery, but architectural clarity.

## In one line

**HAINOA is the open architecture layer that makes the HAIN framework explicit and interpretable.**
